About the library

you are here: home > About the library

Peaceland Institution Library contains both physical (hardcopy) and digital (softcopy) academic resources. The library is one of the largest repositories of academic resources, having the largest eBook database in the world in its Open Access platform, which contains over 2.7 million eBooks, and over 52 million journal articles.

Resources in the library, both virtual and physical, are arranged in a way that enables users to easily locate contents which suit their researches.

The library also enables students and staff to have easy access to their research work once they get them ready. Staff and Students' researches can be accessed both as a hardcopy at the physical library, and as a digital work on the e-library portal by clicking and logging into the School Research platform. Do not hesitate to contact us on library@peaceland.edu.ng whenever you experience any problem accessing the library contents.
