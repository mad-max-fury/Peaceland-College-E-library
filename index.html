Welcome to the eLibrary

Mr. Michael Ugwu
Institution Librarian

Head, Peaceland Central Library, would like to welcome you to this fastest growing and developing institution in South East, Nigeria with a central library saddled with the responsibility of providing quality education through adequate collection of information materials for teaching and learning. We have literatures that cover all disciplines we offer in our institution and beyond. Thus, you are encouraged to make use of these our collections for information, research, education, etc anytime, anywhere and with any internet aid-device for easy accessibility.

However, more than just coming to library to read and borrow a book, we offer information services through our state-of- art facilities and e-library that keep giving readers current literatures published around the world without wasting time. The literature collection comprises: e-books, e-journals, periodicals, reference sources and other information materials. This you can get in the comfort of your home without visiting the central library, though, for registered users. The Management of the Institution and library team will keep expanding our scope and extending frontiers of spectrum of knowledge for learning to a new horizon with global best practices in library and information services.
